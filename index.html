05 / Capstone

The graduation project.

Senior Capstone 2026 ★ Team Leader

HUMEX

An AI occupancy measurement system for smart campuses and facility management.

HUMEX is a computer-vision platform that turns ordinary surveillance cameras into real-time occupancy sensors. Instead of recording footage that no one watches, the system detects people, tracks movement, and surfaces density signals the moment they matter.

I led the end-to-end design: system analysis, requirements definition, architecture, and the interactive dashboard. The pipeline runs on YOLOv8 for detection, a tracking layer for movement continuity, and a React + Streamlit front-end that lets facility managers see what's happening — not just what happened.
